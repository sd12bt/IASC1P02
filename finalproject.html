OUR STORY

TELHOL was founded in 1999 in Montreal, Canada. Our founder Sam created a fun, relaxing, modern space for individuals to enjoy. That included providing amenities that would attract guests to especially enjoy their time at the hotel. Guests that stay at TELHOL can enjoy a modern updated fully equipped full size gym, a luxurious 5-star quality swimming pool, a state of the art roof top patio with bar and dining options available, a five-star restaurant with weekly featured guest chefs, a luxurious ball room for weddings and parties, free cocktail hour from 5 pm to 7 pm each night where refreshments and light snacks are provided, and various organized activities (i.e. board game night, craft night, movie night) to keep solo travelers entertained. Rooms are also equipped with top of the line linens, and in-suite control (i.e. self regulating temperature control and remote control surround sound). Rooms also include full screen TV's, microwaves, mini refrigerators, and the list goes on and on! The TELHOL brand quickly established a name for itself and has expanded into four major cities (New York, London, Los Angeles and Paris). Each location is situated in the downtown cores and are easily accessible via public transit. Our motto at TELHOL is 'Come stay awhile', with amenities like this you'll never want to go home we promise!
